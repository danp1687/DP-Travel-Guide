About us

What is this website?

Welcome to European War Travel, this website is your ultimate guide to exploring the profound historical sites of Europe. Our mission is to connect travelers with the rich and often poignant history of wartime Europe, offering insights that transcend mere tourism. From the beaches of Normandy to the heart of Berlin, we aim to provide an in-depth look at the places that have shaped our modern-day world.

What do we offer?

• Detailed guides to historic sites like the Auschwitz-Birkenau State Museum, Anne Frank House Museum, Normandy Beaches, Berlin Wall, and more.

• Interesting Facts and intriguing stories and lesser-known facts about each location to enhance your visit.

• Articles, photos, and videos that bring history to life, for you to fully immerse yourself in the significance of each site.

Why choose us?

At European War Travel, we are dedicated to providing a deeper understanding of Europe’s wartime heritage. Our content is meticulously researched and thoughtfully presented to ensure you have a meaningful and memorable experience. Whether you are a seasoned traveler or planning your first historical journey, we are here to support you every step of the way.
